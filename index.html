About Me


I am Jui Soshte. My friends call me Luna. I am a Multi-Disciplinary Designer based in Mumbai. I love painting, books, poems, cats and coffee. I initially started sketching anime characters as a hobby and eventually started experimenting with different media. I enjoy being around nature and take inspiration from it. I aspire to be more creative with my designs and to explore things that are outside of my comfort zone.
